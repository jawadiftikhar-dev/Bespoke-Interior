01 What types of projects does Bespoke Interior Studio undertake?
Bespoke Interior Studio works across the full spectrum of architectural typologies — private residences, cultural institutions, hospitality environments, corporate headquarters, and landscape interventions. We are driven by complexity and context, not scale alone. Whether a 200m² retreat or a 40,000m² civic complex, each project receives the same depth of intellectual and creative investment.
02 How does the initial consultation and design process work?
We begin with a Discovery Dialogue — an unhurried conversation (typically 90 minutes) in which we listen before we speak. From there, we develop a contextual brief, followed by a Concept Phase featuring spatial studies, material explorations, and atmospheric mood boards. Each phase is collaborative, iterative, and paced to your needs. We never rush what must be considered slowly.
03 What is the typical timeline for an architectural project?
Timelines vary significantly by project type and scale. A residential commission typically spans 14–24 months from first meeting to final key-handover. Institutional projects often range from 3–6 years. We provide detailed phase-by-phase schedules during the brief stage, and our project architects maintain weekly communication to ensure nothing is lost to ambiguity.
04 Does Bespoke Interior work internationally & fees structured?
Yes — we are currently active across 18 countries and have dedicated site-presence protocols for international commissions. Fees are structured as a percentage of construction cost for full-service projects, or on a fixed-fee basis for consultancy and concept-only engagements. We are fully transparent about fee structures from the first meeting and provide detailed proposals before any commitment is made.
05 How does Bespoke Interior approach sustainability in design?
Sustainability is not a checklist for us — it is a philosophical commitment embedded from the first sketch. We integrate passive solar design, bioclimatic strategies, locally sourced materials, and embodied carbon analysis at every phase. All projects since 2022 target net-zero operational carbon, and we maintain long-term relationships with ecological engineers and environmental consultants as core studio partners.
06 Can we commission design study without architecture?
Absolutely. Our Interior Architecture division operates both as a standalone service and in concert with structural commissions. From complete material and furniture specifications to lighting design and spatial sequencing, we offer full interior curation for existing spaces that wish to be transformed from the inside out.
